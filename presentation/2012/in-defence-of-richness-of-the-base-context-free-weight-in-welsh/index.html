In defence of Richness of the Base

Context-free weight in Welsh

September 14, 2012

CASTL Decennium Conference, University of Tromsø/CASTL, Tromsø, Norway

 download handout

Categories:  Welsh Substance-free phonology

I briefly present the analysis of mora assignment to consonants in South Welsh. The pattern can be analysed by appeal to the form of the inputs (i.e. with a massive number of accidental gaps, which is undesirable), or by positing a constraint schema with undesirable consequences for factorial typology. I suggest that the latter solution is preferable theoretically, although it may be premature to choose it for that reason alone.
